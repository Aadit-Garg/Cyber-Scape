CYBER SCAPE

Welcome to CYBER SCAPE!


Cyber Scape provides you with an immersive and fascinating experience of modern technology and world affairs. It is an experimental project by Aadit Garg and Tanmay Sharma of XI standard at S. D. Public School, Jagadhri, under the guidance of Mrs. Geetanjli. It packs a powerful chatbot that shows the characteristics of a human with its vocal abilities; it generates a response based on the famous technique of checking and biases. On the other hand, there is a news site, Newsroom-inc, which provides you with up-to-date news and information and updates every half hour to keep you updated. There is also a third website that one can use to get data about any book available online for free.

ML models

A showcase for ML (machine learning) models, one of which tracks yourhand, and the other one can track your body movement. The body model is equipped with modes too. One can track your body really accurately but provides choppy visuals, and the other one is a lot faster but less accurate.

News App

Newsroom Inc. is an app that provides you with the latest news and updates. It works by using an API to get post requests, and updates itself every half-hour or so. Stay informed with every tap!

Books

A website which integrates the use of an API and clever backend code to show details as well as provide the link to the book's store. You can buy the paperback version or can download the E-book. Enter as a Guest, Leave as a Storyteller.

Chatbot

A chatbot which can mimic human vocational style made with the help of PaLM model provided by google.
