Hand Model

Hand Model is a model made with the help of python and mediapipe it accurately track at most two hands at a time and provides accurate visuals. It also tracks bending motion of hands and their position. It have multiple use cases in real and commercial world.

  • It can be used for touchless controlling and can help in no sterile use cases.

  • It can help in controlling possibly dangerous equipments like toaster or gyser for visually impaired.

Body model

Body model is a model made with the help of python it takes in the use of opencv(computer vision) it is made possible by the model trained by tensorflow which is a free trained model bank provided by Google it consits of two models lighting and thunder model. lighting model is faster and provides with somewhat 30-45FPS but is less accurate. Whereas thunder model provides with abot 20-23FPS but is very accurate. It have multiple applications in real world.

  • It can help people keep track of their posture.

  • It can help people to make work on their body posture and language.

  • It can provide with body posture data for research.

  • It can provide you with feedbacks with your body posture while doing workout or yoga etc.
