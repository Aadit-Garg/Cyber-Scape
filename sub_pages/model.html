Hand Model

Hand Model is a model made with the help of python and mediapipe. It accurately tracks at most two hands at a time and provides accurate visuals. It also tracks bending motion of hands and their position. It has multiple use cases in real and commercial world.

  • It can be used for touchless controlling and can help in no sterile use cases.

  • It can help in controlling possibly dangerous equipments like toaster or gyser for visually impaired.

Body model

Body model is a model made with the help of python. It takes in the use of OpenCV(Computer vision). It is made possible by the model trained by Tensorflow which is a free trained-models bank provided by Google. It consits of two models lighting and thunder model. Lighting model is faster and provides around 30-45 FPS but is less accurate; whereas thunder model provides around 20-23 FPS but is very accurate. It has multiple applications in real world.

  • It can help people keep track of their postur during actions like walking or swimming.

  • It can help people to improve their body posture and language.

  • It can provide body posture data for research.

  • It can provide you with feedbacks of your body posture while doing workout or yoga etc.
